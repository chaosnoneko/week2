Daifuku

A classic Japanese mochi

Ingredients

For the cake

  • Mochiko flour
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Strawberries
  • Anko
  • Potato/Corn starch

Method

  1. Combine mochiko flour with sugar and water
  2. Stir the mixture on the stove until you get a sticky texture
  3. Place the ready mixture on a plate covored with potato/corn starch . Leave to cool
  4. When the mochi is cooled down you can make balls out of it
  5. Spread the balls in to a circle shape like a plate
  6. Place anko inside and then put the strawberry
  7. Close the mochi so no anko and strawberry will be visible and you are done
